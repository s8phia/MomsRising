Health Resources

Physical Health

  • Canadian Mental Health Association: This organization offers mental health support and resources for all Canadians, including single mothers. They have branches across Canada that offer counselling and support groups.
  • Health Canada: This government agency provides information on a range of health topics, including nutrition, mental health, and child health. They also offer information on health services and programs that are available to Canadians.
  • Canada Prenatal Nutrition Program (CPNP): This is a federal program that provides funding to community-based programs to support the health and well-being of pregnant women facing challenging life circumstances. The program aims to improve access to prenatal nutrition, increase the chances of having a healthy pregnancy, and promote healthy birth outcomes.
  • Women's Shelters Canada: This organization provides resources and support to women and their children who are fleeing domestic violence. They have a directory of shelters across Canada that provide safe housing and support services.
  • Canadian Red Cross: This organization provides a range of health and wellness services to Canadians, including emergency services, first aid training, and community health programs. They also offer support services for refugees and new immigrants.

Mental Health

  • Single Moms, Fresh Start: This non-profit organization offers resources and support for single mothers in Canada. They provide access to counselling services, educational resources, and job training.
  • The Single Mom's Support Network: This organization offers support, advice, and resources to single mothers in Canada. They provide help with budgeting, parenting, and accessing community resources.
  • Canadian Mental Health Association: This organization offers mental health support and resources for all Canadians, including single mothers. They have branches across Canada that offer counselling and support groups.
  • Kids Help Phone: This organization provides free, confidential counselling and support for children and youth across Canada. They offer phone and online chat counselling services, as well as a variety of resources on mental health, relationships, and other topics.
